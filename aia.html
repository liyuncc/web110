A redesign or brand new website? Brand new

Client's goals Help recruit new students and store information for training manuals and videos.
Audience Information
Audience Issues Approach
Potential members:
The target population is high-school graduates and college students who like to try out new things with a gender focus on 60% of males and 40% of females. They probably own a laptop or a desktop plus mobile devices and have moderate computer skills using both Mac and PC platforms with access to cable and WIFI Internet connection.
  • Limited income
  • Little knowledge about historical fencing
  • Just want to try it out and don't want to commit to it
  • Interested in what kind of equipment is needed
  • Marketing strategies: How to make fencing look cool to them
  • Offering 1st lesson free and special arrangement if they can't afford the tuition
  • Gear borrowing system; offering informational and educational videos and documents
  • Providing the client's contact info
  • A page of links to makers and stores that make or sell equipments
  • Presenting fencing videos, pictures and events highlights.
Established members:
The target population is an age group from 22 to 45 with almost 100% of males. Likely they just start their career or already have a steady job which brings income with diverse educational backgrounds (from high school graduates to people with advanced degrees). They probably own a laptop or a desktop plus mobile devices and have moderate computer skills using both Mac and PC platforms with access to cable and WIFI Internet connection.
  • Logistics: Tuition and due date; class updates or cancellation notifications; upcoming events
  • How to improve their fencing
  • Having posts to handle those logistics; providing the client's contact info.
  • Offering resources to instruction videos, fencing manuals and links.
Visitors from other schools:
The target population is people from other fencing schools with diverse backgrounds in age, education, jobs and incomes. Possible gender Composition: 80% of males and 20% of females. They very likely have basic to moderate computer skills. In addition, they probably own a laptop or a desktop plus mobile devices using both Mac and PC platforms with access to cable and WIFI Internet connection.
  • They want to know about what type of school it is and its philosophy; is it welcoming?
  • Logistics: Hours, location and cost
  • Armour requirements
  • Rule sets
  • A statement section: "Who we are", our mission and philosophy
  • Offering 1st lesson free and special arrangement if they can't afford the tuition; providing the client's contact info
  • A "Q & A section" to answer questions about the armour requirements and rule sets
